P-value: 0.014

(With significance level = 0.05)
We reject the null hypothesis. At least one section has a different mean than the other groups, with statistical significance. There's a 1.41% chance of a Type I error.

F-statistic: 2.315

A larger F-statistic means more difference between groups.
An F-stat of 2.315 means that the variance between sample means is 2.315 times the variance within samples.
